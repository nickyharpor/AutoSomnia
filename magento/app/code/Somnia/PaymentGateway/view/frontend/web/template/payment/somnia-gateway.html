Pay with Cryptocurrency (SOMI)

After placing your order, you will be redirected to our secure payment gateway to complete your cryptocurrency payment. The payment process is simple:

  1. You'll be redirected to the payment gateway
  2. Send the exact SOMI amount to the provided address
  3. Wait for blockchain confirmation
  4. You'll be redirected back to the store

Note: Your order will be processed once the payment is confirmed on the blockchain.
